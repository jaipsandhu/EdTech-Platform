Here is a comprehensive design prompt detailing the UI/UX style, visual language, and component structure based on the provided website screens. This can be used as a brief for a designer or as a prompt for UI generation. ### **Design System: Ventriloc** **Overall Theme & Art Direction** * **Vibe:** Modern, minimalist, corporate B2B, clean, and data-driven. * **Style:** Typography-centric, utilizing ample white space, high-contrast layouts, and subtle, sophisticated interactive elements. It balances professional austerity with bold accent graphics. --- ### **1. Color Palette** * **Primary Background:** Pure White (`#FFFFFF`). * **Secondary Background (Footer/Dark Modes):** Deep Charcoal / Off-Black (approx. `#1C1C1C`). * **Primary Text:** Dark Charcoal / Almost Black (approx. `#1A1A1A`). * **Secondary Text:** Medium Gray (used for placeholders, secondary navigation, and footer links). * **Accent Color 1 (Typography Emphasis):** Earthy Olive Gold (approx. `#8A7045`). Used to highlight specific words in hero headers (e.g., "Together", "Let's visualize"). * **Accent Color 2 (Brand Graphic):** Vibrant Orange (approx. `#FF6B35`). Used for large, abstract brand shapes (e.g., the large bracket/brace in the footer). --- ### **2. Typography** * **Font Family:** A clean, modern geometric sans-serif (similar to *Inter*, *Roobert*, or *Helvetica Neue*). * **Hierarchy & Styling:** * **Hero Headers (H1):** Very large, bold to medium weight. Uses tight line height. Often mixes dark charcoal text with the Olive Gold accent color for emphasis. * **Section Headers (H2/H3):** Small, all-caps or title case, clean and unobtrusive (e.g., the small "Contact" or "Data visualization" text above hero headlines). * **Body Text:** Regular weight, highly legible, medium gray or dark charcoal depending on the background. * **Links/Navigation:** Regular weight. Hover states in dropdowns use a dark gray rectangular highlight behind the text. --- ### **3. Layout & Grid** * **Structure:** Primarily utilizes a split-screen (50/50) or asymmetric two-column layout for hero sections (e.g., Text on the left, forms or dashboard mockups on the right). * **Spacing:** Generous padding and margins. High utilization of negative space to draw focus to the typography and core elements. * **Width:** Content is contained within a max-width wrapper, keeping edges aligned across different sections. --- ### **4. UI Components** * **Buttons:** * **Shape:** Fully rounded (pill-shaped). * **Style:** Solid Dark Charcoal/Black with white text. * *Example:* "Contact us" in the nav, "Let's work together" in the hero, Submit button in the form. * **Navigation Bar:** * Clean, transparent background (blending with the white page). * Logo on the far left. * Centered links with subtle downward chevrons indicating dropdowns. * Right-aligned utility section: Language toggle ("IN") and a pill-shaped CTA button. * **Dropdown Menus:** * Floating white cards with a very soft, diffused drop shadow. * Clean categorization with small, bold headers. * Hover state: A dark charcoal box encapsulates the active link text with white font. * **Forms:** * Ultra-minimalist. * No bounding boxes or background colors for input fields. * Uses only a thin, light gray bottom border. * Labels act as placeholders within the field structure. * **Toggles/Switches:** * Pill-shaped track. Light gray background with a white or dark thumb indicator (seen in the footer for language selection: FR / IN). * **Icons & Graphics:** * **Icons:** Thin line, minimalist style. (e.g., circular checkmarks for lists, simple chevrons). * **UI Mockups:** Software/dashboard visualizations are presented as floating white cards with soft drop shadows, keeping the internal UI clean and aligned with the brand's aesthetic. * **Brand Graphics:** Large, flat-color, abstract shapes (like the orange right-brace in the footer) extending off-screen to create visual interest in otherwise dark/empty spaces.
